Braatpaq Case

The Braatpaq bag is designed to look like your traditional laptop carrying case and is perfect for most laptops.

Description

The classic look of the Braatpaq bag retains simplicity by keeping available storage pockets to a minimum. It is made with durable dernier includes an external pocket with a Velcro cover. It features a zipper that remains in position – the zipper will not inadvertently open if it was only partially closed.

The Braatpaq bag measures 15in x 13in x 4in to comfortably accommodate 12 to 15.4 inch laptops and comes with two adjustable shoulder straps for those who may want to use it in their vehicles. The clamshell design provides an added option where allowed so you don't have to remove your laptop at airport security checkpoints. It also makes it easy to add papers, stationery and business cards to the few available pockets.

The simplicity of the Braatpaq brief case is what makes it special but its versatility is a bonus feature. The bag can easily be setup in most cars to allow rear seat passengers to watch the laptop screen without having to hold it. Click on the link to see a video of how it's done.
