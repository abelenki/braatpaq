Braatpaq Case

BRAATPAQ(r) is a carrying case that allows rear seat passengers to enjoy mobile video on a laptop computer in a vehicle.

The BRAATPAQ is easy to use and works in most vehicles with no tools required.

With the BRAATPAQ, you can avoid the expense of a mounted unit in your vehicle.

Car Adaptor

The Car Adaptor converts the 12-volts from your car's accessory socket to 110-volts. Plug this in for hours of uninterrupted video in your car as it keeps your laptop charged. Price $30.

Audio Cassette Adaptor

Use the cassette adaptor when you want your laptop's sound to be heard on your car stereo. Price $19.99

Wireless FM Adaptor

The FM Adaptor is an FM transmitter that is useful for those cars that are not equipped with a cassette player. You simply plug it in to your laptop and tune your radio to the matching FM station. Price $21.99

Stereo Audio "Y" Splitter Adaptor

With this cable, you can hook up 2 (two) head phones to the laptop computer so those in the front seats do not hear what is playing on the laptop. Price $7.99
